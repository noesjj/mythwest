It Ain't Over Yet

Casual Outdoor Outerwear & Equipment

At Mythwest, we dream of a world where nature isn't just a place to visit, but a part of life. We're all about getting people stoked about the outdoors, providing them with practical gear and rad clothing that helps them soak up the raw beauty of nature.

We're all about showing love for the environment and teaching others how to care for it too. Our goal is to build a fun, diverse outdoor community where everyone feels welcome, from first-time hikers to seasoned adventurers.

Mythwest's vision is simple: make awesome stuff that makes outdoor life better, keep Mother Earth happy, and inspire folks from all walks of life to get out there and experience the wild side. Together, we'll create a Mythwest full of excitement, connections, and a shared love for the great outdoors, leaving a legacy of adventure and conservation for the next generation.
